Welcome to Sunset Park Food Coop.We share responsibilities and benefits equally. We strive to be a responsible and ethical employer and neighbor. We offer a diversity of products with an emphasis on organic, minimally processed and healthful foods. Our commitment to wholesome foods includes selling only organic produce, focusing on local foods, and providing vegan and gluten-free products.Our dedication to convenience and service means our store is open when you need it and staffed with helpful and knowledgeable community members.
